Frequently asked questions

  • What age groups do you cater to in your art classes?
    We teach students from age 4 to adults.
  • How do you customize the curriculum to match individual student needs?
    During the class, we continuously assess students' current skills and learning pace, engaging in discussions with them about the topics being covered.
  • Can I join your classes as a complete beginner, or do I need prior experience?
    Whether you're a complete beginner or already have some experience in drawing or painting, you're welcome to join our classes. We'll start from the basics for beginners and provide guidance tailored to your existing skills if you're already familiar with drawing or painting.
  • Do you offer workshops and events?
    Yes, we occasionally host workshops and events. Stay updated on our upcoming events by keeping an eye on our announcements in Facebook.
