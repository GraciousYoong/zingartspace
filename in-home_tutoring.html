In-Home Tutoring

Ideal for students with higher budgets seeking one-on-one exclusive services. We will dispatch professional teachers to your home or specified location for classes. Limited to specific areas within Klang Valley.

Benefits

Personalized Instruction

In a one-on-one setting, the tutor can tailor the lessons specifically to the student's skill level, interests, and learning style. This personalized attention allows for more effective learning and faster progress.

Flexible Schedule

Home tutoring allows for flexibility in scheduling sessions. This is particularly advantageous for students with busy schedules or those involved in other activities.

Individualized Feedback

The tutor can provide immediate and detailed feedback on the student's artwork, helping them understand their strengths and areas for improvement.

Focus on Specific Areas

Whether it's painting, drawing, sculpture, or any other medium, home tutoring allows the student to focus on specific areas of interest or improvement without the distractions of a group setting.

Enhanced Motivation

With individualized attention and encouragement from the tutor, students may feel more motivated to practice and improve their artistic skills.

Convenience

Home tutoring eliminates the need for travel to and from art classes, saving time and energy for both the student and their parents.

Parental Involvement

Parents can easily monitor their child's progress and communicate with the tutor, ensuring that the lessons align with their child's goals and expectations.
